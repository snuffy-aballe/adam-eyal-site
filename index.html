What We Do

We sing original songs to strangers on the street.

Adam is American. Eyal is Israeli. They perform in English, Hebrew, and broken everything else. They approach people in public with a guitar and sing them a song about their life — bold lyrics, real reactions, no script.

The format is what makes it work: the stranger's genuine reaction is the content. It's comedy built around whoever's in front of them. On the street, on stage, at your event — the audience becomes part of the act.

They've been featured in an ARTE documentary about Jewish identity, covered in the Berliner Zeitung, and have performed sold-out shows across 50+ cities worldwide.
